Request for Money services business (MSB) and Foreign money services business (FMSB) registration

From: Financial Transactions and Reports Analysis Centre of Canada (FINTRAC)

FINTRAC is modernizing its tools and processes to serve you better. This form is intended for businesses operating as MSB and FMSB.

What would you like to do with your registration (required)

Renewal request

How to renew your registration:

Step 1:

  • Send a renewal request to FINTRAC using this form.
  • In your renewal request, you must indicate if you require changes in your registration information.

Step 2:

  • FINTRAC will validate the information submitted.
  • If additional information is needed from you, FINTRAC will contact you (via email, telephone, mail or other).
  • In the event that you need to change any of the MSB registration information, FINTRAC will contact you (via email, telephone, mail or other).

Step 3: You will receive a confirmation of the renewal.

Cessation request

How to cease / cancel your registration:

Step 1: Send a cessation request to FINTRAC using this form.

Step 2:

  • FINTRAC will validate the information submitted.
  • If additional information is needed from you, FINTRAC will contact you (via email, telephone, mail or other).

Step 3: You will receive a confirmation of the cessation.

Change request

How to change your registration:

Step 1: Send a change request to FINTRAC using this form.

Step 2:

  • FINTRAC will validate the information submitted.
  • If additional information is needed from you, FINTRAC will contact you (via email, telephone, mail or other).

Step 3: You will receive a confirmation from FINTRAC once the change has been made.
